🔍 Dashboard Insights
Revenue by Category: Women's category leads with ~2M revenue, followed by Men, Winter, Ethnic, Sports, and Bottom wear
Revenue Contribution by State: Tamil Nadu tops with 14.33% share, followed closely by Uttar Pradesh (13.66%), Rajasthan/Gujarat/Delhi (~13% each), and Karnataka (9.86%)
Category Count by State: Tamil Nadu and Karnataka have the highest product category counts, followed by Rajasthan, Delhi, and Madhya Pradesh
Monthly Sales Trend: Quantity sold fluctuated through the year, peaking in April (~300 units) with a dip in February and December
Top Products by Quantity Sold: Men's T-Shirts led sales (400 units), followed by Dress, Saree, Top, and Jacket
Top Products by Revenue: Men's T-Shirts generated highest revenue (0.5M+), followed by Top, Dress, and Saree
🧰 Features
KPI cards for quick summary of revenue, units sold, categories, and products
Donut chart for state-wise revenue contribution
Horizontal bar chart for category distribution by state
Line chart for month-wise sales trend (Jan–Dec)
Bar charts for category-wise and product-wise revenue ranking
